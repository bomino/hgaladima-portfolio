## Teaching

Developed and taught 40+ course offerings across graduate and undergraduate programs at Old Dominion University, Eastern Virginia Medical School, Virginia Commonwealth University, and St. Cloud State University. Current courses at ODU include MPHO 605 (Introduction to Biostatistics), MPHO 712 (Applied Biostatistics), MPH 612, MPH 616, MPH 713, and CHP 711/445.

Built [**Z-t-Chi Calculator**](https://ztchi.hgaladima.com/) — open-source biostatistics toolkit used in MPHO 605 and available to any instructor.
