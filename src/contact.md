## About the Z-t-Chi Calculator

If you have a question about the [Z-t-Chi Calculator](https://ztchi.hgaladima.com/) — a bug report, a feature request, adopting it for your own course, or access to the instructor builder at [teach.hgaladima.com](https://teach.hgaladima.com/) — email me with "Z-t-Chi" in the subject line.

## Statistical consulting

I provide **pro bono statistical consulting** through [Statistics Without Borders](https://community.amstat.org/statisticswithoutborders/home) for organizations in resource-limited settings. For paid research collaborations or methodology consulting, email directly.

I also provide ongoing biostatistical support (*pro bono*) to the ODU Center for Global Health, Chesapeake Health Department, and Suffolk Public Schools.
